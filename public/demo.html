How it works

Visual flows and Discord interface mockups for Valorant OCE Utilities — a Riot account verification and rank-tracking bot serving the OCE community.

01 — Account Verification

RSO-powered account linking

Players link their Riot account to their Discord identity using Discord's OAuth connections. The bot verifies ownership via RSO — no passwords, no third-party credentials.

01

Admin sets up the verification panel

An admin runs /admin link panel to post a persistent "Link Account" button in a channel. Members interact with this to begin verification.

02

Player runs /link with their Riot ID

The bot looks up the account via the Riot API and generates a unique OAuth state token. A verification link is sent privately to the player's DMs.

03

Player authorises via Discord OAuth

The player clicks the link, which redirects to Discord's OAuth screen. The bot reads their connected Riot Games account from Discord's connections API.

04

PUUID verified — rank fetched — role assigned

The bot confirms the PUUID of the connected account matches the Riot ID provided, then fetches competitive rank and assigns the correct rank role in the server.

05

Ongoing re-validation every 6 hours

The bot re-checks every linked account's Discord connections automatically. If a Riot connection is removed or swapped, the link and rank role are revoked and the player is notified.

02 — Player Commands

Rank, match data & leaderboard

Once linked, members can view their rank, match history, and how they stack up against the rest of the server.

/profile

Player Profile

Shows your current rank, peak rank, K/D, win rate, and top agents from the last 20 matches. Includes a Unicode sparkline showing rank progression over time. Self-only — use public: True to post it visibly in the channel.

Ephemeral
/privacy

Privacy Toggle

Toggle your visibility on the public /leaderboard ranked list. Hidden accounts are still counted in the rank distribution chart (anonymous). Re-run to flip the setting.

Ephemeral
/match latest

Latest Match

Full breakdown of the most recently completed match — agent, K/D/A, ACS, HS%, and a team-by-team score table.

Ephemeral
/match history

Match History

Last 10 matches at a glance with per-match result, agent, K/D/A, ACS, and an overall W/L record for the set.

Public
/unlink

Unlink Account

Removes your linked Riot account, revokes all rank roles, and clears your rank history. Requires re-verification via /link to restore access.

Ephemeral
/leaderboard

Server Leaderboard

Paginated ranked list of all verified server members sorted by competitive tier and RR. Immortal+ members show their live leaderboard position. Your own position is pinned below the page when you're not on the current page.

03 — Web Dashboard

Live rank distribution

The public dashboard at valorant-oce-utilities.vercel.app displays a real-time breakdown of the server's rank distribution across all verified members — updated automatically every 6 hours.

Server rank distribution — Iron → Radiant
01

Bot generates stats on startup and every 6 hours

The bot reads the cached_rank field for all linked members and computes a per-tier distribution. Results are written to Upstash Redis under the key stats:rank-distribution.

02

Vercel serverless endpoint serves the data

A serverless function at /api/stats reads the Redis key and returns JSON — the web page fetches this on load with no direct database access required.

03

25 bars rendered — Iron 1 through Radiant

Each bar is coloured by rank group with percentage labels and hover tooltips showing exact member counts. The chart includes total linked, ranked, and unranked member counts.

04 — Moderation & Admin

Staff tools for server management

Administrators have a full suite of moderation tools for managing linked accounts, enforcing verification integrity, and auditing activity.

01

/admin link get — look up any member's link

View a member's linked Riot account, rank, region, link date, and full audit history of changes to their link.

02

/admin link remove — revoke a link

Manually remove a member's verified link and rank role. All actions are written to the audit log with moderator attribution.

03

/admin link bulk-reset — season resets

Force re-verification for all members of a given role — used at the start of each Valorant act to refresh rank data across the server.

04

Duplicate link attempt logging

When a user attempts to claim a Riot account already linked to another Discord member, the attempt is logged to the staff channel with the claimant, the Riot ID, and the current owner.

05

Background account validation

Every 6 hours the bot re-checks all linked accounts via Discord's connections API. Account swaps and disconnections are detected automatically with a full scan summary posted to the staff log channel.

05 — Voice Channel Management

/lock and /unlock — Comp & Squad VC control

Any member inside a designated Comp or Squad voice channel can lock it mid-game to prevent new players joining, then unlock it when the session is over.

01

Member runs /lock from inside the VC

The bot denies @everyone Connect on the channel and grants an individual Connect: true overwrite to every member currently inside — preserving their access.

02

10-minute reconnect window on disconnect

If a locked-in member disconnects (crash, drop, etc.), their overwrite is preserved for 10 minutes. Rejoining the channel cancels the timer. Not rejoining removes their access automatically when the window expires.

03

Member runs /unlock to reopen the channel

Resets the @everyone overwrite and removes all individual member overwrites added during the lock. Cancels any active reconnect timers. Works correctly even after a bot restart by inspecting live channel state rather than relying on in-memory data.

04

Restricted to designated channels

Commands are scoped to the 26 Comp and Squad voice channels on the main server. Any member in the VC can run /lock or /unlock — no special role required.
