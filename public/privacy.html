Valorant OCE Utilities

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Valorant OCE Utilities (the "Bot") collects, uses, and stores information when you use its services within the Valorant OCE Discord server.

By linking your Riot account through the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you link your Riot account, we collect and store the following:

  • Discord User ID — your unique Discord identifier
  • Riot Account Information — your Riot ID (name and tag), PUUID (Riot's unique account identifier), and region
  • Rank Data — your current and peak Valorant rank, cached periodically to reduce API calls
  • Rank History — a timestamped record of your rank changes over time, used to display progression charts in /profile
  • Match Statistics — aggregated stats (K/D, win rate, top agents) derived from your recent match history, cached for performance
  • Discord OAuth Tokens — temporary access and refresh tokens issued by Discord during the verification process, used solely to re-verify your Riot account connection during periodic background scans. Tokens are stored securely and never used for any other purpose.
  • Link History — a record of when your account was linked and any previous accounts linked to your Discord
  • Audit Logs — administrative actions performed on your account (e.g. resets) including the moderator, reason, and timestamp

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the Bot's features:

  • Verifying ownership of your Riot account via Discord OAuth2
  • Assigning rank roles in the Valorant OCE Discord server
  • Displaying your profile stats and rank progression via /profile
  • Enabling server leaderboards — your Riot ID and rank are visible to all server members via /leaderboard
  • Generating the public rank distribution chart on the website, which displays anonymised aggregate data only
  • Periodically re-verifying your Riot account connection to maintain link integrity
  • Maintaining moderation records for server integrity

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured SQLite database hosted on our bot server. Rank and stats data is cached temporarily and refreshed periodically. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorised access.

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with the following third-party services to function:

  • Discord API — for authentication, role assignment, and messaging. Governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • Riot Games API / RSO — to verify and retrieve Riot account data. Governed by Riot Games' Privacy Notice.
  • HenrikDev Unofficial Valorant API — used to retrieve rank and match data. This is an unofficial API wrapper.

5. Data Retention

Your linked account data is retained for as long as you remain linked. You may delete your data at any time by running /unlink, which removes your Riot account association, rank history, and cached statistics from our database. Discord OAuth tokens are refreshed automatically and expire in accordance with Discord's token policies. Audit log entries may be retained for a reasonable period for moderation purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View your stored data via /profile and /lookup (staff)
  • Remove your linked account data at any time via /unlink
  • Request manual data removal by contacting a server moderator

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. By using the Bot, you confirm that you meet Discord's minimum age requirements in your country.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact the Valorant OCE server moderation team via the server's ticketing system or a moderator directly.
